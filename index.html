Our Story

A Legacy of Fresh & Finest Seafood

Established in 2012, Eshwar Sea Foods has grown to become one of Hyderabad's most trusted seafood dealers and suppliers. We take immense pride in sourcing the freshest catches directly from coastal fishermen and reputed importers, delivering premium quality to your table.

From households seeking fresh fish for daily meals to five-star hotels and large catering operations requiring bulk supply, we cater to every need with the same commitment — uncompromising freshness, hygiene, and quality.

Our product range spans everything from premium Grade-1 prawns in multiple sizes to exotic imported fish, local favourites, lobsters, crab, and crab meat — all handled under strict cold-chain and hygiene protocols.
